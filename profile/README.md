# SillyLittleTech

## About 
We are dedicated to providing accessible website creation tools, templates, and open hosting platforms. Our goal is to lower the technical and financial barriers to personal web publishing, ensuring everyone, regardless of ability or resources, can build a high-quality, accessible online presence. As a fiscally sponsored project of Hack Club, we operate solely as a non-profit initiative to provide these digital public goods at no cost to the community.

For help setting up a project based on these templates, reach out to me @ `kiya [dot] rose [at] sillylittle [dot] tech`
